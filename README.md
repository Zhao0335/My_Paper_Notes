# My_Paper_Notes

I'm a freshman majoring in Artificial Intelligence. This repository is used to organize my paper reading notes in the AI field, mainly in the form of extensive reading, aiming to accumulate knowledge and improve my academic reading ability.

## Language Versions

This repository contains notes in both Chinese and English versions:
- **中文版**: Located in the `中文版` directory, contains paper notes in Chinese. See [Paper_List_CN.md](Paper_List_CN.md) for the list.
- **English Version**: Located in the `English Version` directory, contains paper notes in English. See [Paper_List_EN.md](Paper_List_EN.md) for the list.

Each note follows a standard template structure. You can find the template in [English Version/Template.md](English%20Version/Template.md).

## Reading Goal

Master the core basic papers in the field of artificial intelligence, cultivate the ability of extensive paper reading and academic analysis, accumulate cutting-edge research ideas and technical methods, and assist in the study of professional courses and the completion of relevant tasks.

## Welcome to Communicate

As a freshman, I have a limited understanding of academic papers, and there may be mistakes or omissions in the notes. I sincerely welcome seniors, teachers and friends in the field to put forward suggestions, corrections or discussions. Let's learn and progress together!
