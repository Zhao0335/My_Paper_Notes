# My_Paper_Notes

I'm a freshman majoring in Artificial Intelligence. This repository is used to organize my paper reading notes in the AI field, mainly in the form of extensive reading, aiming to accumulate knowledge and improve my academic reading ability.

## Reading Goal

Master the core basic papers in the field of artificial intelligence, cultivate the ability of extensive paper reading and academic analysis, accumulate cutting-edge research ideas and technical methods, and assist in the study of professional courses and the completion of relevant tasks.

## Note Format

### [Extensive Reading]

#### [Paper Background]

Briefly describe the research background, existing problems and research significance of the paper.

#### [Innovation Points]

- **Idea Innovation**: The innovative research ideas or perspectives proposed in the paper.

- **Technical Innovation**: Innovative technologies, algorithms or frameworks designed in the paper.

- **Experimental Innovation**: Innovative experimental design, evaluation methods or data processing methods.

#### [Core Methods]

Focus on the core algorithms, models or technical routes of the paper, and briefly sort out the implementation logic.

#### [Experimental Setup]

Such as datasets used, evaluation metrics, baseline models, hyperparameter settings, etc.

#### [Experimental Results]

Analyze the experimental results around the research questions (RQ1, RQ2, ...), and briefly summarize the key conclusions.

#### [Conclusion and Expectation]

Summarize the core conclusions of the paper and the future research directions proposed by the authors.

### [Reading Gains]

Record the knowledge, methods or inspirations gained from reading the paper, as well as the deficiencies that need to be improved.

## Welcome to Communicate

As a freshman, I have a limited understanding of academic papers, and there may be mistakes or omissions in the notes. I sincerely welcome seniors, teachers and friends in the field to put forward suggestions, corrections or discussions. Let's learn and progress together!
